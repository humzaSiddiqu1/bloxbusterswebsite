FAQ

What is Bloxbusters?

Bloxbusters is an independent community project aiming to deliver a Hytale-inspired game experience. We’re building a home for arcade games, an Elden Ring-style MMO and a collaborative movie studio.

Is Bloxbusters affiliated with Hytale?

No, we are fans and creators; Bloxbusters is not affiliated with Hytale or Hypixel Studios.

How can I get involved?

Join our Discord community to follow development, provide feedback and eventually contribute to content creation or playtesting.
